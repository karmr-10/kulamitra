# **App Name**: Kulamitra

## Core Features:

- Splash Screens: Display initial splash screens depicting the application's purpose with a next button.
- Member Directory: A searchable directory of all community members, with basic profile information pulled from registration.
- Background Watermark: Keep a dark watermark of Vasavi Maatha, the community goddess of Arya Vyshya community, on all app screens.
- Sign-up and Sign-in: Separate sign-up and sign-in for members and admins, with Google account sign-in and Firebase authentication.
- Event Calendar: Display upcoming community events, meetings, and festivals in a calendar format with notifications/reminders.
- RSVP and Attendance Tracking: Allow members to RSVP to events and track attendance using QR codes.
- Announcement Broadcasting: Admins can send announcements to all members or specific groups (e.g., youth, women).
- Year-wise Event Summary and Gallery: Year wise event summary with gallery of the evets conducted
- Event Report Generation: Option for the admins to generate a report of any event or meetings conducted
- Volunteer Coordination: Allow members to volunteer for events.
- Payment Options: Enable payment for donations with multiple payment options and ChitFunds with unique user IDs.
- AI-Powered Community Summary: Use an AI tool to provide regular summaries of community activities based on events, announcements, and other activities. Useful to enhance engagement for those who can't attend all activities.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep saffron (#FF9933) to evoke the traditions and vibrancy of the community.
- Background color: Soft beige (#F5F5DC) to create a warm and inviting feel.
- Accent color: Burnt orange (#CC6633) to highlight key actions and information.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for headlines and short amounts of text; body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for longer text.
- Use culturally relevant icons to represent different aspects of the community and its activities.
- Maintain a clean, card-based layout for easy navigation and content consumption.
- Subtle animations for transitions and feedback to enhance the user experience.
